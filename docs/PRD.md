# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Project Name

LinkForge

## Tagline

Forge Better Links.

---

## Vision

LinkForge is a modern link management platform that helps users create, manage, and track shortened links through a clean, fast, and secure interface.

---

## Problem Statement

Long URLs are difficult to share and impossible to track effectively.

Existing URL shorteners either:
- feel outdated,
- hide useful analytics behind expensive plans,
- or lack a modern user experience.

LinkForge aims to provide a simple, transparent, and developer-friendly alternative.

---

## Target Audience

- Students
- Developers
- Freelancers
- Content Creators
- Small Businesses

---

## Goals

- Fast URL shortening
- Easy link management
- Beautiful dashboard
- Useful analytics
- Privacy-focused experience

---

## Core Principles

- Privacy First
- Simplicity
- Transparency
- Speed
- Open Source
